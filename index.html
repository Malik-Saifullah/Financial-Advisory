Upload a Bank Statement

1. Download your electronic statement

Visit your online banking website and download an electronic statement for your bank or credit card account in any of these file types:

  • .ofx Microsoft Money
  • .qbo QuickBooks
  • .qfx Quicken
  • .aso Simply Accounting
  • .csv CSV file

2. Upload your statement to Wave

Once you have your electronic statement downloaded to your computer, upload it to Wave using the form below.

Select the account for which you're uploading a statement, or add a new account.

    Cant find the account youre looking for? Create an account using the instructions below:

  • Click "+" beside Payment Account drop-down menu
  • Find savings and checking accounts under Asset > Bank > Bank & Cash; you may create a custom account by selecting Other Bank Account
  • Find credit cards and lines of credit under Liability/Credit Card > Current Liability; you may create a custom account by selecting Other Current Bank Debt
Having trouble uploading a CSV? Visit our help article to download a CSV template and to fix common errors.

3. Manage your transactions

After you upload the file, Wave will add the transactions in the file to the Imported Transactions page. Learn more.
